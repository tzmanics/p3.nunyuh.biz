*avoid using special characters like: " , ' , < , > as they may interfere with the HTML code.>



  • PAGE INFO
    • Page Title:

      The PAGE TITLE shows up in the tab of the web browser. Consider this when deciding the length of the title. This will also show up as a header on your page.



      Keywords:

      KEYWORDS go in the meta tag and help people find your page within search engines.*

      *separate each keyword with a comment

  • NAVIGATION
    • Other Pages:




      These PAGES will show up as links that users can click to take them to that page. Make the other HTML pages and save them with the name you put in these text fields (i.e. yourPageName.html. Make sure these files are in the same directory as this file so the browser can find them :)

  • CONTENT
    • Article 1 Title:
      Article 1 Content:

      Article 2 Title:
      Article 2 Content:

      The ARTICLE TITLEs will show up as a bold header for the ARTICLE CONTENT that make up the, bulk content of your site.



  • FOOTER
    • Page Creator:

      The PAGE CREATOR is the person or company name you want to show at the bottom of the page.



      Contact Email:

      This will be the email address a user can click to email you.
